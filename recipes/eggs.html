Scrambled Eggs

Scrambles Eggs

This is a great dish. Eggs are fantastic when scrambled.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan on medium heat.
  2. Once heated, crack all three eggs straight into the pan and scramble. Don't stop moving the eggs while theyre cooking.
  3. Put in your seasoning.
  4. Serve when fully cooked. Garnish with parsley.
